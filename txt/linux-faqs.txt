
 linux-faqs (Linux FAQ collection from ibiblio.org) 
 
 Frequently Asked Questions (and answers!) on many Linux topics. 
 
 Some of the FAQs included in this package: 
 ATAPI-FAQ, AfterStep-FAQ, BLFAQ (Brief Linux FAQ), Ftape-FAQ, 
 GCC-SIG11-FAQ, Joe-Command-Reference, the Linux-FAQ, PPP-FAQ, SMP-FAQ, 
 and the Threads-FAQ. 
 
 These FAQs will be installed under /usr/doc/Linux-FAQs/.
