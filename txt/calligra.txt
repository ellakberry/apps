
 calligra (KDE office productivity suite) 
 
 Calligra office productivity applications. Included are a word 
 processor, spreadsheet, presentation program, chart and graph creator, 
 vector graphics tool, database report creator, flowcharting program, 
 integrated data management program, a pixel graphics editor, 
 a mathematical formula editor, and Calligra Workspace 
 (a combination of all of the above). 
 
 For more information, see: http://calligra-suite.org/
