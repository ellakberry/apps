
 sysstat (System performance monitoring tools) 
 
 The sysstat utilities are a collection of performance monitoring 
 tools for Linux. These include the iostat, mpstat, pidstat, sadf, 
 and sar tools. 
 
 The sysstat utilities were written by Sebastien Godard. 
 
 For more information, see the sysstat web site: 
 http://pagesperso-orange.fr/sebastien.godard/
