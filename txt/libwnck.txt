
 libwnck (Window Navigator Construction Kit library) 
 
 libwnck is the Window Navigator Construction Kit, a library for use 
 in writing pagers, tasklists, and more generally applications that 
 are dealing with window management. It tries hard to respect the 
 Extended Window Manager Hints specification (EWMH). 
 
 libwnck is used by compiz to provide the gtk-window-decorator program.
