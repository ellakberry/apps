
 xpaint (simple X paint program) 
 
 XPaint is a versatile bitmap/pixmap editing tool with a feature set 
 similar to MacPaint. 
 
 The short features list: 
 Brushes, Spray paint, Pencil, Lines, Arcs, Pattern Fill, Text, 
 Boxes, Circles, Polygons. 
 Works on multiple images simultaneously. Cut/Copy/Paste between all 
 active images. Fatbits/Zoom on the image windows.
