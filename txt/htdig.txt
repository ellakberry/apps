
 htdig (web indexing and searching system) 
 
 The ht://Dig system is a complete web indexing and searching system 
 for a small domain or intranet. This system is not indended to 
 replace the need for outside Internet search engines such as Google, 
 but rather to cover the search needs for a single company, campus, or 
 even a particular sub section of a web site.
