
 snownews (a console RSS newsreader) 
 
 Snownews is a console RSS/RDF news reader. It supports all versions 
 of RSS natively and can be expanded via plugins to support many other 
 other formats. 
 
 Snownews was written and is maintained by Oliver Feiler. 
 
 Snownews homepage: http://kiza.kcore.de/software/snownews
