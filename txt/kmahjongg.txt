
 kmahjongg (KDE port of Mahjongg game) 
 
 KMahjongg is a clone of the well known tile based patience game of 
 the same name. In the game you have to empty a game board filled 
 with pieces, by pairwise removal of pieces of the same type. 
 
 For more information, visit: http://www.kde.org
