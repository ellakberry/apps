
 taglib-extras (taglib support for other formats) 
 
 Taglib file type plugins which were split off from Amarok 
 to allow others to use and help maintain them. 
 Taglib-extras delivers support for reading and editing 
 meta-data of audio formats not supported by taglib, including: 
 asf, mp4v2, rmff, wav. 
 
 The taglib-extras are part of kdesupport: http://www.kde.org
