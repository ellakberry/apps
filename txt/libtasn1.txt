
 libtasn1 (ASN.1 library) 
 
 Libtasn1 is the GNU ASN.1 library. Abstract Syntax Notation One 
 (ASN.1) is a standard and flexible notation that describes rules and 
 structures for representing, encoding, transmitting, and decoding 
 data in telecommunications and computer networking. 
 
 Libtasn1 was written by Fabio Fiorina.
