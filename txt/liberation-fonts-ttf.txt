
 liberation-fonts-ttf (Liberation TTF Fonts) 
 
 Liberation is the collective name of three TrueType font 
 families: Liberation Sans, Liberation Serif and Liberation 
 Mono. These fonts are metric-compatible with Arial, Times 
 New Roman, and Courier New respectively. 
 
 The fonts were developed by Steve Matteson of Ascender Corp. 
 for Red Hat, Inc.
