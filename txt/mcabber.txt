
 mcabber (Jabber console client) 
 
 mcabber is a small Jabber console client for Linux, maintained by 
 Mikael Berthe. Mcabber includes features such as SSL support, 
 multi-user chat (MUC), history logging, commands completion, and 
 external actions triggers. 
 
 mcabber project home is: http://www.lilotux.net/~mikael/mcabber/
