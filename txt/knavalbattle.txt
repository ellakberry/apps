
 knavalbattle (KDE version of battleship game) 
 
 KNavalBattle is a battleship game with built-in game server. 
 Ships are placed on a board which represents the sea. Players try 
 to hit each others ships in turns without knowing where they are 
 placed. The first player to destroy all ships wins the game. 
 
 For more information, visit: http://www.kde.org
