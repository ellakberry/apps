
 kbounce (KDE Bounce Ball Game) 
 
 KBounce is a single player arcade game with the elements of puzzle. 
 It is played on a field, surrounded by wall, with two or more balls 
 that move about in the field bouncing off of walls. 
 The player can build new walls, decreasing the size of active field. 
 
 For more information, visit: http://www.kde.org
