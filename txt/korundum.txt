
 korundum (KDE ruby bindings) 
 
 Very complete bindings to both the KDE API and the Qt APIs. 
 The Korundum package includes both a QtRuby Qt-only binding along 
 with the full combined Qt/KDE one. 
 The QtRuby package contains just Qt bindings with no dependencies 
 on KDE. 
 
 For more information, visit: http://rubyforge.org/projects/korundum/
