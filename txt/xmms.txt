
 xmms (X Multimedia System) 
 
 XMMS is the X Multimedia System. It is used to play audio and other 
 kinds of media files. By default XMMS can play MPEG audio, Ogg 
 Vorbis, RIFF wav, most module formats, and a few other formats. XMMS 
 can be extended through plugins to play a number of other audio and 
 video formats.
