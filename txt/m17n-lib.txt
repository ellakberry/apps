
 m17n-lib (application multilingualization library) 
 
 M17n is an abbreviation of Multilingualization. The m17n library 
 implements multilingualization for many applications. The m17n 
 library supports functions to handle M-texts. This package also 
 contains the m17n database. 
 
 m17n-lib home: http://m17n.org/
