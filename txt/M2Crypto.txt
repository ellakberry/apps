
 M2Crypto (cryptography toolkit for Python) 
 
 M2Crypto is a crypto and SSL toolkit for Python. It includes: 
 - RSA, DSA, DH, HMACs, message digests, symmetric ciphers (e.g. AES) 
 - SSL functionality to implement clients and servers 
 - HTTPS extensions to Python's httplib, urllib, and xmlrpclib 
 - Unforgeable HMAC'ing AuthCookies for web session management 
 - FTP/TLS client and server, S/MIME v2, ZServerSSL, ZSmime
