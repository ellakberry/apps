
 bovo (five-in-a-row board game) 
 
 Bovo is a Gomoku ("five points" in Japanese) like game for two 
 players, where the opponents alternate in placing their respective 
 pictogram on the game board. Also known as: Connect Five, 
 Five in a row, X and O, Naughts and Crosses. 
 
 For more information, visit: http://www.kde.org
