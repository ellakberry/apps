
 libmcrypt (MCrypt encryption library) 
 
 Libmcrypt is a data encryption library. The library is thread safe 
 and provides encryption and decryption functions. This version of 
 the library supports many encryption algorithms and encryption modes. 
 Some algorithms which are supported: SERPENT, RIJNDAEL, 3DES, GOST, 
 SAFER+, CAST-256, RC2, XTEA, 3WAY, TWOFISH, BLOWFISH, ARCFOUR, WAKE, 
 and more. 
 
 Homepage: http://mcrypt.sourceforge.net
