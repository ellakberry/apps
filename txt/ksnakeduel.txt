
 ksnakeduel (simple snake duel game for KDE) 
 
 KSnakeDuel is a simple snake duel game for KDE. You can play 
 KSnakeDuel against the computer or a friend. The aim of the game is 
 to live longer than your opponent. To do that, avoid running into 
 a wall, your own tail and that of your opponent. 
 
 For more information, visit: http://www.kde.org
