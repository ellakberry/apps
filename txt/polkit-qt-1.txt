
 polkit-qt-1 (Qt polkit API wrapper) 
 
 polkit-qt-1 aims to make it easy for Qt developers to take advantage 
 of the polkit API. It is a convenience wrapper around QAction and 
 QAbstractButton that lets you integrate those two components 
 easily with polkit. 
 
 Homepage: http://techbase.kde.org/Polkit-Qt-1
