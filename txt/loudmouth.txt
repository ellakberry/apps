
 loudmouth (XMPP C programming library) 
 
 Loudmouth is a lightweight and easy-to-use C library for programming 
 with the Jabber protocol. It is designed to be easy to get started 
 with, and yet extensible enough to allow you to do anything that the 
 Jabber protocol allows. 
 
 loudmouth home: http://www.loudmouth-project.org/
