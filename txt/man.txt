 kapman (KDE version of Pac-Man) kap
 kap
 Kapman is a clone of the well known game Pac-Man. kap
 You must run through the maze to eat all pills without being captured kap
 by a ghost. By eating an energizer, Kapman gets the ability to eat kap
 ghosts for a few seconds. When a stage is cleared of pills and kap
 energizer the player is taken to the next stage with slightly kap
 increased game speed. kap
 kap
 For more information, visit: http://www.kde.org kap
 
 man (format and display the on-line manual pages) 
 
 The man package is a collection of tools used for searching and 
 reading the online system documentation. In fact, on most UNIX-like 
 operating systems it is the primary means of finding out how programs 
 on the system work. For example, 'man man' will display the 
 documentation for man itself. 
 
 Man requires the groff text processing package.
