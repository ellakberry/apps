
 moc (console audio player) 
 
 MOC is a console audio player with a simple ncurses interface. 
 It supports OGG, FLAC, WAV, and MP3 formats. Just run mocp, go to some 
 directory using the menu, select a file, and press enter to play it. 
 MOC will continue playing the rest of the files in the directory. 
 
 MOC was written by Damian Pietras. 
 
 Homepage: http://moc.daper.net
