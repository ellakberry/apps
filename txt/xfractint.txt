
 xfractint (fractal generation program) 
 
 Xfractint draws a wide variety of fractals. Xfractint is a port of the 
 DOS program Fractint. 
 
 The primary original authors of Fractint are Bert Tyler, Timothy 
 Wegner, Mark Peterson, and Pieter Branderhorst. Fractint was ported 
 to X by Ken Shirriff.
