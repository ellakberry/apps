
 wqy-zenhei-font-ttf (Wen Quan Yi Zen Hei CJK Font) 
 
 The WenQuanYi Zen Hei font is a Chinese (or CJK) outline font with 
 Hei Ti style (a sans-serif style) Hanzi glyphs. This font is 
 developed for general purpose use of Chinese for formating, printing 
 and on-screen display. This font is also targeted at platform 
 independence and the utility for document exchange between various 
 operating systems. 
 
 wqy-zenhei-font-ttf home: http://wqy.sourceforge.net/en/
